LeJOS Robot

For Design Project class. With a team of 4 designed a leJOS robot running on Java to localize in a grid, navigate an unknown maze and shoot ping-pong balls at targets given before a run.

Created website for the McGill EV formula electric design team. The site was made to showcase the various vehicles, promote sponsors and display contact information.

Musical Glove

For highschool art project. Using the Arduino Uno board, developped a glove that played notes and switched octaves when touching your thumb to parts of your finger.
